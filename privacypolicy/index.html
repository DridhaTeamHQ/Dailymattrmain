Who We Are

DailyMattr (“DailyMattr” or the “App”) is a news and infotainment platform owned and operated by Dridha Technologies Private Limited, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, with its office at Madhapur, Hyderabad, Telangana, India – 500081 (“we”, “us”, “our” or the “Company”). For the purposes of the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (the “DPDP Act”) and the Digital Personal Data Protection Rules, 2025 (the “DPDP Rules”), the Company is the Data Fiduciary for personal data processed through the App.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use, share and protect it, how long we keep it, and the rights available to you. It applies to everyone who uses the App, whether or not you create an account, and should be read together with our Terms of Use. Words used but not defined here have the meaning given to them in our Terms of Use, the DPDP Act and the DPDP Rules.

Consent and Your Choices

We process your personal data on the basis of your consent, except where the law permits processing for certain legitimate uses. At or before the point of collection, we provide you with a notice describing the personal data we seek, the purposes for which it will be processed, how you may withdraw your consent, and how you may exercise your rights or make a complaint.

Your consent is sought through a clear affirmative action. You may withdraw your consent at any time, and doing so will be as easy as giving it. Withdrawal will not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal. If you decline to provide, or withdraw consent to, personal data that is necessary for a feature of the App, we may be unable to provide that feature to you.

  • Approximate location – we collect your approximate (not precise) location to show you relevant local news and infotainment content.

  • Phone number – used to create and secure your account and to verify you at login. Registration is required only if you wish to post comments or use certain features such as the AI Assistant; you can read content without registering.

04

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data only for the following purposes:

  • to provide, operate, maintain and improve the App and its features, including the AI Assistant, and to present content to you effectively;
  • to create and secure your account and to enable you to post comments;
  • to show you relevant local content based on your approximate location;

AI Assistant

The App may make available an artificial-intelligence assistant feature (the “AI Assistant”) that you can use to explore, summarise or ask questions about news and infotainment content. The following terms apply when you use it.

6.1 Data we process through the AI Assistant

When you use the AI Assistant, we process the prompts, questions and other inputs you submit, together with the responses generated, in order to provide, secure and improve the feature. The AI Assistant may be powered in whole or in part by third-party artificial-intelligence service providers acting as our processors. Where this is the case, your inputs may be processed by, and transferred to, such providers in accordance with Section 8 (Sharing and Disclosure) and Section 9 (Cross-Border Transfers). We do not use your AI Assistant inputs to serve you targeted advertising.

You should not submit to the AI Assistant any passwords, financial account details, special-category or sensitive information, or personal data relating to other individuals. You are responsible for the inputs you provide and should not rely on the AI Assistant as a means of storing information.

6.2 Acceptable use of the AI Assistant

The AI Assistant is provided for lawful, personal and reasonable use in connection with the App. You agree that you will not use the AI Assistant to:

  • engage in any unlawful, fraudulent, harmful, harassing, abusive, defamatory or infringing activity;
  • generate or disseminate misinformation, or content that impersonates any person or entity;
  • generate content that is obscene, hateful, sexually explicit, or harmful to minors;
  • attempt to reverse-engineer, extract or copy the underlying models, prompts or training data, or to circumvent any usage limits, content filters or security measures;
  • access the AI Assistant through automated means such as bots or scrapers, or use it to send spam or bulk or automated content;
  • overload, disrupt, degrade or impair the AI Assistant or the infrastructure that supports it; or
  • use the AI Assistant for any purpose other than its intended use, or in a manner that is unnecessary, excessive, or that places an unreasonable burden on our systems.

We may monitor use of the AI Assistant for security, abuse-prevention and compliance purposes, and we may impose usage limits on, or restrict, suspend or withdraw access to, the AI Assistant (in whole or in part) where we reasonably believe it is being misused or used in breach of this Policy or our Terms of Use.

6.3 No reliance on AI Assistant outputs

The AI Assistant generates responses automatically, and its outputs may be inaccurate, incomplete or out of date. Its outputs do not constitute professional, legal, financial, medical or other advice, and you should not rely on them as such. You are responsible for independently verifying any information before acting on it. The disclaimer in Section 17 applies equally to AI Assistant outputs.

We retain your personal data only for as long as it is needed for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.
