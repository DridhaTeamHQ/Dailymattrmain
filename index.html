What is Long Mattr?

Long Mattr is Mattr’s premium newsletter experience for deeper stories, useful context, and curated perspectives.

What will I receive?

Daily case studies and weekly shorts across your chosen categories, written to be read in minutes.

Do I need an account to subscribe?

No — just your email address. You can create an account later to manage preferences.

How often will I get newsletters?

You choose: daily, weekly, or both. Change the cadence anytime from any edition.

Can I choose my categories?

Yes — pick from General, Real Estate, Automobile, Health & Wellness, Tech & AI, and Markets & Startups.
